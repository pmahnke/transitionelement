--- layout: travel title: "Thailand : Chaing Mai to Sukothai ~ Day 9" --- h1. Thailand h2. Chaing Mai to Sukothai ~ Day 9 p. _HOT HOT HOT 99F 100% HUMIDITY_ p. We took a plane to Sukothai and saw this amazing ancient capital that is really ruins in a lovely park. Apparently was only discovered from the air, they saw some lines in the jungle that suggested walls. The whole place is a mass of temples, but was really the heart of a palace and a city. Really worth the trip. ||| ||| ||| ||| || p. We met a wonderful English couple that we spent a lot of time with just wandering around. They told us all about the south, and Koh Samui. p. We spent the night attempting to get a ride to the airport in the morning. ( _Sanam bin kwrap?_ ) We got one and hung out in a really local night market that was a lot of fun to walk around in. Thailand comes alive at night. Its really the only cool time of the day. We bought quite a few good snacks and little presents for home.
