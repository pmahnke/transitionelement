For three years we have been trying to get to this corner of Holland to see the tulips. All plans had been scuttled (alternate *work* holiday, missed ferry boat, etc...) But we finally made it, albeit a little late for the proper 'in the fields' season. But the Keukenhof Gardens were at their peak. What a wonderful trip.

**River of Flowers**

<a href="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_flowerriver.jpg"><img src="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_flowerriver.jpg"  alt="River of Flowers" /></a>

There were a couple of these around the park. I can't even imagine how they planted them all.

**Hyacinth**

<a href="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_hyacinth.jpg"><img src="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_hyacinth.jpg"  alt="Hyacinth" /></a>

There were loads of fragrant hyacinth around the park.

**Red & Yellow Tulips**

<a href="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_mixed.jpg"><img src="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_mixed.jpg"  alt="Mixed Tulips" /></a>

The majority of the flowers at Keukenhov are tulips, over 7 million bulbs have been planted.

**Deep Purple**

<a href="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_purple.jpg"><img src="https://media.transitionelement.com/assets/images/keukenhov_purple.jpg"  alt="Purple Tulips" /></a>

I just loved the masses of dark flower heads.

**Tulip Fields**
