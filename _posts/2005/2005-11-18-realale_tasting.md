### New Forrest Perry

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| Rating (out&nbsp;of&nbsp;5) | 1 1/2 |
| Brewer | BJ & SD Topp  <br>Littlemead  <br>Burley, Ringwood  <br>Hants BH24 4ED |
| Region | South England |
| ABV | 7%  |
| Type | Perry (Pear) |
| Tasting notes | Light straw in colour with a lovely pear aroma. Both sweet and dry in taste with strong floral aftertaste. If you like cider, you will like this. Great with chicken, fish or thai/chinese food. |
