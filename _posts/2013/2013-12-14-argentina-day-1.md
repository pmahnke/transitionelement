We made it!  95f feels a bit different to London right now. 

Today we flew to Buenos Aires via Madrid.  We took a cab to our hotel, which was being converted to a hotel actively when we arrived. (I note it now appears to be closed).  Our rooms were big and done to a really high standard.  We took a good nap and then wandered around the Palermo Soho area.
