We made it!  95f feels a bit different to London right now. 

This morning we arrived around 8:30am in Buenos Aires. We took a cab to our hotel, which was actively being converted to a hotel when we arrived. (I note, it now appears to be closed).  Our rooms were big and done to a really high standard. The first thing we did? Got in touch with our money changer, Patricio. We did two transactions with him in the first two days.  We got a great rate and a fancy bottle of red.  He got a lot of perfect USA one hundred dollar bills.

We took a good nap and then wandered around the Palermo Soho area.
