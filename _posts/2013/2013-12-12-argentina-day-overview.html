We started and ended in Buenos Aires, but spent the bulk of the trip in Chubut and Santa Cruz provinces – Patagonia. We visited the Valdes Peninusla with its sea lions, elephant seals, whales and penguins, then Punta Tomba for the penguins. Then we headed inland to Sarminento and the Bosque Petrified Forrest and then down Ruta 40 to Cuevas del las Mano and finally El Chaltan and El Calafate. From there we took a bus to Chile and did the ‘W’ hike in the Torres del Paine park.
