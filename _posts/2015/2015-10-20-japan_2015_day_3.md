This morning we got up early (not early enough for the tuna market at 4:30am or something crazy) and went to Tsukiji Fish Market (note: this market has moved, but there is still the outer market for tourists).

The market is a live, active market. You can walk around it, but you are just in the way of people doing there jobs. But we did anyway. We were a bit surprised at the high-end vegetables for sale, specially the very fancy mushrooms, which were everywhere.

We visited the outer market as well and ate a bunch of nice foods.

Then we visited the [Yasukuni-jinja Shrine](https://maps.app.goo.gl/jWCZ7739yrsRbmYg6) where the autumn festival was on.
