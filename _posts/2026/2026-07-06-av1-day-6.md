| **Stayed** | Rifugio Tissi |
| **Hiking** | 18.96 kilometers - 8:13 hours - 2,259 meters ascent |

The hike started with a bit of a long scrambley hike across scree and boulder fields of Mount Pelmo. Then a sharp climb up to the Forcella di Col Negro. 

We stopped for lunch at Refugio Coldia. 

Then we walked around the Lago Coldia and down the deep Civetta Valley until we saw Refugio Tissi, high on a massive.

We climbed the sharpely vertical trail to the refugio and enjoyed amazing views, Canasta, and a fantastic sunset.

We had a private room, but it was pretty tiny and featureless.

A long, but rewarding day.
