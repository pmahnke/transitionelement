Today we walked around the Joo Chat neighbourhood. 

We started in a Muslim focused hawker centre on the day before Eid, the end of Ramadan. Most of the stalls were busy selling food for the big celebratory dinner. It was fun to see. 

Then we wandered down through the neighborhood and checked out cool old homes, street art and fancy shops.

After a good a explore, we headed back centrally and had a Singapore Sling at the Raffles Long Bar, where the drink was invented.
