After a 14 hour flight, avoiding the Iran and Ukrain wars, we arrived the following evening in Singapore. 

It was still light and 25C when we arrived. The metro station that had an easy connection was closed, so we had to go a longer route, but found our way easily enough.

We checked in and went to a really nice Chinese meal in our building and then headed off to bed.

It was also Angela’s birthday!
