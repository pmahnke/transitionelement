There was a lot more illegal construction and noise all night, we complained and asked if they could move us.

Today we walked through Little India and visited several budhist and hindu temples.  It was not touristy at all.  We had an excellent, cheap lunch in a local hawker centre. 

In the afternoon we visited the Muslim area, visited a mosque and had some 'pulled tarka tea'. It was a few days day before Eid, you could feel the excitment of the community.  Many parks had tents set up to have a communial breaking of the Ramadan fasts. There were also a lot of people selling food to people driving by in cars, and giving away a lot of food as well.

After walking around for a while, we went up a big tower for some rooftop drinks and sunset.  The views of the city and harbour are amazing.

After a drink, we went back to the Muslim area and dinner at a Turkish place.
