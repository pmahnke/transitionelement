| **Stayed** | Rifugio Biella |
| **Hiking** | 14.54 kilometers - 5:30 hours - 670 meters ascent |

After a simple breakfast, we headed out along the Fosses plateau and then down into the Perderu basin.  It was a very steep downhill.  We stopped for a strudel and skiwasser at Rifugio Perderu.

Then it was a big up to Vallon de Mareo and Rifugio Fanes.  We had another private room here, with its own shower.

Dinner was with our Spanish friends and it was another lovely day.
