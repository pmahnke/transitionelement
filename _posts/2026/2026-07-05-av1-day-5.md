| **Stayed** | Rifugio Città di Fiume |
| **Hiking** | 14.76 kilometers - 6:33 hours - 1339 meters ascent |

Today was a long walk from Nuvolau over Passo Giau to Rifuguio Città di Fiume.

The walk down was a bit confusing to start, you need to walk down the road behind the chair lift at Rifuguio Averau.  We knew this, but still missed the correct turning.  With only a bit of a back track, we got it.  At Passo Giau, there was a big cycle race that we stopped to watch and had a drink.  

Then we crossed the road and walked along the lovely Mondeval plateau, with vast, flowered alpine meadows. 

We climbed over the Forcella Ambrizzola, with amazing views of the sheer face of Monte Pelmo, before a final long descent through larch forests to the our rifguio.

There we played cards with Sam and Aoife and enjoyed the sunset and storms in the distance. 

A long, but lovely day.
