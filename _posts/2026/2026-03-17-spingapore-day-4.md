Today we did a eight kilometer hike of the southern ridges, a trail through four parks. 

It was lovely, but very hot. The views were impressive, there were two really nice bridges that were as much art as useful. And the parks and suburbs along the way all very clean and nice. 

We took a bus back and spent the afternoon and evening in Chinatown eating at the Maxwell Hawker Centre, drinking green tea and shopping.  In the evening, we had drinks a cool bar, Sago, and then hotpot for dinner.

A very enjoyable day.
