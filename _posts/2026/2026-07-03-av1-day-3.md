| **Stayed** | Rifugio Lagazuoi |
| **Hiking** | 13.28 kilometers - 6:41 hours - 1,200 meters ascent |

This morning started with a brief and sharp up to Fanes Plateau.  Then a pretty level walk along a valley.

Then another steep climb up Forcella del Lago with Lago di Lagazuoi so steeply below that you have to climb down hundereds of switchbacks to even see it.

After what felt like an hour of going down, you are at the pretty lake, where we stopped for lunch and a break.

Then another up to a very steep set of switchbacks to Rifugio Lagazuoi.  Along the way are a dozen or so WWI tunnels and fortifications used to monitor troops coming into Italy.

At the top, the views were amazing. The whole of the central Deformities is before you.  We spend hours looking at it all.

As there is a gondola station here, it was very busy until 4pm, when the lifts start to slow down and stop at 5pm.  We had another private room with a balcony.

Dinner was excellent and we watched the mountains until late.
