| **Stayed** | Rifugio Carestiato |
| **Hiking** | 10.64 kilometer - 5:58 hours - 1,268 meters ascent |

Today was a dangerously hot hike.

It started with a steep drop to the valley floor, then a walk to Refugio Vazzoler, where we stopped for a drink.

Then more down until we started to walk across the fingers of Mount Moiazza's watershed.  It was exposed and very hot.  We drank our five litters of water and I nearly had heat stroke.  I really suffered.

At the hut I drank another four liters of water, but cramped heavily for a long time.

I didn't sleep well, and didn't recover well.  However, we reunited with our Spanish friends and a couple from North Carolina that we meet on the bus to the start of the hike.
