| **Stayed** | Rifugio Biella |
| **Hiking** | 7.11 kilometers - 4:40 hours - 971 meters ascent |

Today was pretty warm.

The hike starts with a lovely walk around Lago di Braies and then a long hike up to Sora Forno Pass onto a plateau.  Almost immediately over the pass is Rifugio Biella, a small and old school rifugio.

We got there and grabbed some lunch and almost immediately there was a long thunderstorm.  We sat in the dinning room and met some people we would keep meeting for several days. Two of these where Rachel and Ines, who were very grateful that Angela spoke Spanish.

When we checked in, we had a two person room, which is nice.  We grabbed a shower and took a quick nap.

The evening was lovely and we met AJ at dinner who we would walk with to the end.

A great first day, especially as we missed that storm.
