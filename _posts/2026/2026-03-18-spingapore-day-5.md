Today we headed back to Marina Bay and spent a good few hours in the TeamLab exhibition at the Art & Science Museum. It’s very interactive and fun and a bit different then the one we went to in Tokyo.

Afterwards we went to the National Museum and learned about the history of Singapore. 

For dinner, we ate Mexican food at a place on Clark Quay.
