Today we traveled from London to Cortina, via Venice to get ready for our hike on the Alta Via 1.

We flew from Gatwick.  Arriving in Venice, we had to sort out leaving a bag at left luggage of things Angela would need for her K2 Basecamp hike, that she would have to leave from immediately after our hike.

Then we had to get on a bus to Cortina.  It all went very smoothly.  Soon we were heading up, out of the 'heat dome' covering Venice.  But it was still hot.

We stayed at the famous, Hotel De La Poste.  Cortina just hosted the Winter Olympics, and was very pretty.  We walked around the town, had a few drinks and then a lovely dinner at our hotel.
