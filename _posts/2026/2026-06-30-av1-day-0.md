In the morning, we walked around Cortina again. We didn't want to get to Lago di Braies too early.

We took two buses to get to the lake and arrive a bit before hotel check-in, so we walked around the lake area.  Then we had a coke and pretzel at an outdoor cafe. 

We then checked-in to the hotel, which is 120 years old and very lovely.  We had dinner and breakfast in huge, lovely dinning rooms.  Cocktails on the terrace with free meats and cheeses.

It is also the actual start of the AV1.  While hard to get a reservation, it is the perfect starting point.
