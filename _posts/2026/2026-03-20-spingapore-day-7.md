For our last full day, we headed to the Singapore Zoo. 

While I am not going to recommend this to anyone over seeing things in the city, it was really nice and we were slightly running out of things to do.

The zoo was really well laid out and it was easy to see all the animals. 

After the zoo, we headed to the very high-end shopping on Orchard Street and checked out some Japanese department stores. 

A fun day.
