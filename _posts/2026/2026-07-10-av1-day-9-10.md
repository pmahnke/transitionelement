After a night in Belluno, we took the train to Venice.

From there a boat to Acadamia where we were staying with a view of the bridge.

We mooched around the town for two days and ate and drank well.

It was our third trip to Venice, so we did walk to San Marco Square, but didn't need to go in the buildings.

It was hot, but lovely.
