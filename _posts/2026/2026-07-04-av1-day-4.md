| **Stayed** | Rifugio Nuvolau                                  |
| **Hiking** | 12.21 kilometer - 5:16 hours - 550 meters ascent |

Today's hike was a big down, followed by a big up.

We reversed our path down the switchbacks of Lagazuoi and then down further to road in the valley below.

We did a side hike along Mount Averau, but were stopped by two sets of women warning us to turn around. Eventually we did, but it was still impressive for the kilometer or so we did.

At the bottom there was long walk in the woods on both sides of the road.

Then we cheated and took a gondola up to Bain de Donnes, with a perfect view of the famous Cinque Torri.

After lunch, a wurst roll at Rifugio Scoiattoli, we walked up to Rifugio Averau, where we hung out a bit. The we walked up to the very picturesque Rifugio Nuvolau, at the top of another massive. It was visually so pretty. We had a beer and strudel and then just sat admiring the views.

When the day hikers left, it was lovely and quiet.
