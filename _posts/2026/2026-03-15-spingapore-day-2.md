There was a load of illegal construction right below us, so we had a poor nights sleep.

We were up pretty early and started at the National Gallery. It had many very nice exhibits in old colonial buildings. 

Then we walked to the botanical glass houses called, Gardens by the Bay.  We started going up a Supertree, man-made trees that provide a great view of this part of Singapore harbour.  Then we toured two domes. One had plants and trees from around the world, but had a very nice special exhibition on the Japanese cherry blosson season. The second dome was a highlight of the Singapore rainforests.  It also had a Jurasic Park exhibit.  It was lovely.

At twilight we headed back to the Supertree grove and saw the light show. Then we walk back towards Marina Bay and heard a huge Disney firework display, but could not get to a view point before it ended.  Then we got caught up in the rush to get back to our hotel and had a long wait for a bus. 

It was a busy day with over 22k steps.
