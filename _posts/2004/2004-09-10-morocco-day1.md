*Flight delayed nine hours…*

When we arrived at Heathrow the plane so delayed that they wouldn’t even check us in — might not even be a flight at all.

Two hours later we learned there was a problem with the aircraft and a different plane was coming, but four hours late — **at 10pm** — with no information on our connection to Fez.

Finally at 11pm we took off and arrived at 1:30am. Then after a painfully confusing negotiation with the airline staff of about 30 minutes, they finally took about 60 of us to a hotel in downtown Casablanca for the night — another 30 minutes away. We were finally asleep by 3:30am or so — headed to 2 1/2 hours of sleep — nice.
