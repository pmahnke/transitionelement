Today we travelled to Marrakech by train.  It was an easy jouney overall and interesting to see the countryside a bit.

We stayed at [Riad Bab Firdaus](https://www.angsana.com/morocco/riads-collection/riad-bab-firdaus), right off the medina.  It was a small but lovely old house with a courtyard and plung pool.

We spent the afternoon wandering around the medina and poking our head in the baazar.
