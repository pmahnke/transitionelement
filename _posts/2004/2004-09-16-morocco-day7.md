What a journey. The road to Toubkal in the High Altas Mountains is crazy.  A bus had recently plunged off the road killing everyone.

However, we made it to Imlil and stayed in the [Kasbah du Toubkal](https://www.kasbahdutoubkal.com/), A lovely hotel, high up on a hill, surrounded by the mountains.

Dinners and breakfasts are communal, and they help arrange activities during the days.
