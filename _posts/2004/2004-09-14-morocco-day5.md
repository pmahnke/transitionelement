Today we explored the baazar with a guide. It is such a tangle of alleys, you really almost need one.  Our guide wasn't great, but we felt slightly more confident of getting lost and could ask loads of questions about how things work, like the chicken markets, the herbal medicines, the welding shops and communal ovens.

It was all pretty chaotic and interesting.
